We strive to foster a positive and respectful community. Please review the following guidelines to ensure a safe and enjoyable environment for everyone:

  • Content Restrictions:
    Pornography is strictly prohibited. Nudity may be allowed in appropriate contexts, such as artistic or educational content, as long as it respects the dignity and consent of all individuals involved.
  • Substance Abuse:
    Content that promotes, glamorizes, or encourages the use of illegal substances, alcohol abuse, or addiction is not allowed.
  • Violence:
    Content that promotes violence, threats, or encourages harm against individuals or groups is strictly prohibited. This includes, but is not limited to, calls for physical violence, terrorism, or self-harm.
  • Cyberbullying:
    Cyberbullying, hate speech, or harassment based on race, gender, sexual orientation, or other protected categories is not tolerated.
  • Respect for Others:
    All community members are expected to treat others with respect. We encourage constructive dialogue and prohibit any behavior that degrades, harasses, or disrespects others.
